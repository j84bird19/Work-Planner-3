Bird Planner V24 Supply Cost Model

Updates:
- Supply item Product / Cost Info now uses:
  Cost ÷ Amount of Item for That Cost = Cost Per Unit
  Example: $11.99 / 24 ft = $0.50 per ft
- Quantity Remaining moved under Add Inventory and is readonly.
- Add Inventory adds quantity and logs YTD spent using cost per unit × quantity added.
- Quantity Remaining auto-calculates from total inventory added minus invoice supply usage.
- Invoice supply usage charges cost per unit × quantity used and syncs remaining inventory.
